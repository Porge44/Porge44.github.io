#0001

Each Round will have an objective or quest that the Protagonists need to complete in some way. As the Party gathers more information on the current Round's Quest, the Party's 'Current Round' tab will update accordingly. Rounds may also have sub-quests available to Protagonists that meet certain conditions. Sub-quests are not necessary to complete the Quest, but they will provide extra points and prizes for the Party, so they're always recommended.

Most Rounds do not have a time limit in which the objective must be completed; however, if a Party or Protagonist appears to be deliberately stalling The Game for any reason, the Host will punish the Protagonists in question, ranging from added obstacles to Protagonist disqualification.
